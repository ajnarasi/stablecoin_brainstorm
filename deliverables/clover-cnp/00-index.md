# Clover CNP Growth — FIUSD + Agentic Commerce Workstream

**North star**: Increase Clover merchants' e-commerce / card-not-present (CNP) share using FIUSD and agentic commerce.

**Owner**: Product lead, Commerce Hub (crypto/stablecoin + agentic commerce strategy).

**Scope**: 5-phase exploration (problem → JTBD → strategy → validation → build). Three parallel idea tracks:

- **Track A** — Extend `prototype-2-agent-pay` scaffolding with Clover-CNP-specific agentic-commerce ideas.
- **Track B** — Net-new ideas surfaced bottom-up from JTBD work.
- **Track C** — Re-examine the 4 shipped prototypes (yield-sweep, agent-pay, supplier-pay, cross-border) against the Clover-CNP lens. Produce retain / reshape / retire decisions.

**Primary segment focus**: Restaurants (QSR/FSR) and Services (beauty, home, professional). Specialty retail is a watch.
